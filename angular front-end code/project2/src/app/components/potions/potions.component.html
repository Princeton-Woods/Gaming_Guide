Awkward Potion Recipe

An Awkward Potion can be brewed using a water bottle and a single nether wart his potion is the main starting point to making almost every potion. This potion has no effects

Mundane Potion Recipe

A Mundane Potion can be brewed by using any potion material other than nether wart or glow stone dust. It results in a useless potion that gives no effects.

Thick Potion Recipe

A Thick Potion can be brewed by using glow stone dust and results in a useless potion that has no effects

Potion of Healing Recipe

A Potion of Healing can be brewed with a single glistening melon and an Awkward Potion and creates a Potion of Instant Health which restores 4 hearts. This effect can be doubled by adding a single glow stone dust producing a Potion of Instant Health II which restores 8 hearts.

Potion of Fire Resistance Recipe

A Potion of Fire Resistance can be brewed with a single Magma Crean and an Awkward Potion and lasts for 3:00. The duration can be increased to 8:00 by adding a single piece of redstone dust.

Potion of Regeneration Recipe

A Potion of Regeneration can be brewed with a single Ghast Tear and an Awkward Potion and lasts for 45 seconds. The duration can be increased to 1:30 with a single piece of redstone dust or it can be enhanced to a Potion of Regeneration II 0:22 with a single piece of glow stone dust.

Potion of Strength Recipe

A Potion of Strength can be brewed with a single Blaze Powder and an Awkward Potion and lasts for 3:00. the duraction can be increased to 8:00 by addidng restone or it can be enhanced to Strength Potion II which lasts for 1:30.

Potion of Swiftness Recipe

A Swiftness Potion can be brewed with sugar and an Awkward Potion and lasts for 3:00. The duration can be increased to 8:00 by adding redstone dust or it can be enhanced to a Swiftness Potion II which lasts for 1:30.

Potion of Night Vision Recipe

A Night Vision Potion can be brewed with a Golden Carrot and an Awkward Potion and lasts 3:00. The duration of this potion can be increased to 8:00 with redstone dust.

Potion of Invisibility Recipe

A Invisibility Potion can be brewed with a fermented Spider Eye and a Potion of Night Vision and lasts for 3:00. The duration can be increased to 8:00 with redstone dust.

Potion of Water Breathing Recipe

A Water Breathing Potion can be brewed with a Pufferfish and an Awkward Potion and lasts for 3:00. The duration can be increased to 8:00 with redstone dust.

Potion of Leaping Recipe

A Leaping Potion can be brewed with a Rabbits Foot and an Awkward Potion and lasts for 3:00. The duration can be increased to 8:00 with redstone dust or it can be enhanced to a Potion of Leaping II with a duration of 1:30.

Potion of Slow Falling Recipe

A Potion of Slow Falling can be brewed with a Phantom Membrane and an Awkward Potionand lasts for 1:30. The duration can be increased to 4:00 with redstone dust.

Potion of Poison Recipe

A Poison Potion can be brewed with a Spider Eye and an Awkward Potion and lasts for 0:45. The duration can be increased to 1:30 with redstone or it canbe enhanced to a Potion of Poison II which lasts for 0:21.

Potion of Weakness Recipe

A Weakness Potion can be brewed with a fermented Spider Eye and a Water Bottle and lasts for 1:30. The duration can be increased to 4:00 with redstone dust.

Potion of Harming Recipe

A Potion of Harming can be brewed with a fermented Spider Eye and a Potion of Poison or Strength. The potion can be enhanced to a Potion of Harming II with glowstone dust.

Potion of Slowness Recipe

A Slowness Potion can be brewed with a fermented Spider Eye and a Potion of Swiftness or Leaping and lasts 1:30. The duration can be increased to 4:00 with redstone dust or can be enhanced to a Potion of Slowness II which lasts for 0:20.

Potion of the Turtle Master Recipe

A Potion of the Turtle Master can be brewed with a Turtle Shell and an Awkward Potion and lasts for 0:20. A Turtle Master Potion gives Slowness and Resistance. The duration can be increased to 0:40 with redstone dust or it can be enhanced to a Potion of the Turtle Master II and lasts for 0:20.
